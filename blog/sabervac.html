The SaberVac Build

February 2026 In Progress

Category Design Challenge
Printer Bambu Lab A1 Mini
Electronics Bambu Lab vacuum kit (battery, board, switch, filter)
Release Planned for Makerworld

What It Is

A compact handheld desk vacuum in a custom 3D printed shell. Bambu Lab sells a vacuum kit — battery, control board, switch, and filter — and you either grab an existing shell design from Makerworld or build your own. I did both.

Started by printing a popular existing design to see how the kit goes together. It worked fine, but I wanted to see if I could make it smaller. As the shell got more compact and cylindrical, it started looking like a lightsaber. So I leaned into it.

Status Updates

Early February 2026
Printed an existing Makerworld vacuum design to understand the kit components and how they fit together. Worked, but bulky.
Mid February 2026
Started designing a more compact shell from scratch. Focused on minimizing dead space around the motor and battery. The cylindrical form factor emerged naturally from the component layout.
February 2026 — Current
Working prototype assembled. Noticeably more compact than the popular designs. Top shroud pops off to access internals and empty the dust cup. Battery, motor, and filter all fit cleanly inside.

What's Next

Refining the shell fit and testing different nozzle attachments. Once the final version feels right, this one goes on Makerworld. You can source similar vacuum kits from Amazon if you don't have the Bambu Lab one.
