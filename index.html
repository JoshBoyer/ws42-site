About Workshop 42

Where curiosity meets a cutting mat.

Workshop 42 is a real workshop and the online home for everything I build. I'm a lifelong tinkerer who loves turning ideas into tangible, easy-to-build projects.

Expect a mix of 3D-printable parts, laser-cut files, and simple DIY solutions — everything designed for everyday practicality and fun experimentation.

This site is where I document the process. Short updates, build photos, stuff that worked, stuff that didn't. Follow along or grab something useful.
